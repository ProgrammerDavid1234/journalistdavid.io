Mateo Kovacic Chelsea transfer latest: Price tag revealed, Man City talks, Man Utd hijack

Mateo Kovacic is expected to leave Chelsea this summer, and there are plenty of clubs trying to secure his signature.
The 29-year-old midfielder has been heavily linked with an exit from Stamford Bridge lately, with his contract set to expire in 12-months. football.london understands that the Blues are preparing for his departure, with Bayern Munich and Manchester City said to be interested. It's believed that there's not yet been talks of an extension on his current deal in west London, meaning the Croatia international will become a free agent at the end of next season. Thomas Tuchel is said to be eager to reunite with Kovacic, who performed excellently alongside the German during their time together at Chelsea. Pep Guardiola is also understood to be a big admirer, with the Premier League champions plotting a swoop. Price tag revealed With a move seriously on the cards, The Times have claimed that Chelsea will likely demand between £30million and £40million for Kovacic. The report adds that he would prefer a move to the Etihad Stadium. United hijacking Despite his unimpressive form this season, Rio Ferdinand has urged his former side to ditch their pursuit of Frenkie de Jong and signed Kovacic instead. Speaking on his YouTube channel, FIVE, the 44-year-old said: "It sounds too difficult and too complicated [to sign De Jong] – move on. I think Kovacic has got a year left. I like him… He's different to what we've got."
